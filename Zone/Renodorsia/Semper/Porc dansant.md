Le porc dansant est l'une des tavernes de [[Semper]]. Elle est connue pour ces grandes chambres accueillant anciennement les explorateurs de [[Rendorsia]]. Désormais, le porc dansant accueille les voleurs, et ceux voulant passer [[Semper]] de façon plus discrete afin de rester inaperçu.
Le porc dansant consiste d'un bâtiment a trois niveau, le rez de chausser, la cave et l'etage. au rez de chausser il y tout ce qu'une taverne a besoin, une cuisine, un bar, des tables et les accommodations necessaires. A l'etage, de nombreuses chambres meuble les corridors. Certaines chambres sont plus grande et spacieuses, ces chambres était autre fois celle d'aventuriers célèbres avant la [[Grande guerre de Rendorsia]]. La cave de la tavern est la où tout est entreposé.

Un accès aux [[Égout de Semper]] existe dissimulé derrière un énorme tonneaux de [[Golden Walley]]. L'entré est bloquer par une porte DC 17.

The Dancing Pork is a notorious tavern in the heart of [[Semper]], with a reputation as a refuge for those who prefer to go unnoticed. Its weathered wooden sign, featuring a grinning pig mid-dance, sways gently in the wind, inviting travelers, rogues, and shady characters alike. Once a place where brave adventurers and explorers from [[Rendorsia]] gathered, the tavern now attracts a more secretive crowd seeking shelter from prying eyes.

The tavern is divided into three levels: the bustling ground floor, the quiet upper floor, and the shadowy cellar. The ground floor is alive with the clatter of mugs, the murmur of hushed conversations, and the smell of roasting meat. A long, sturdy bar runs along the back wall, manned by a bartender who seems to know more than he lets on. Scattered wooden tables are worn from years of use, and the dim lighting adds to the tavern’s clandestine atmosphere.

Upstairs, a maze of corridors leads to numerous rooms, each with its own history. Some rooms are small and cramped, perfect for those seeking only a night’s rest, while others are more spacious, once home to famed adventurers before the [[Grande guerre de Rendorsia|Great War of Rendorsia]]. These larger rooms still bear traces of their former grandeur, though the occupants now are far less noble.

The cellar is a different story altogether, rarely visited by patrons. Here, crates and barrels are stored, but it is whispered that the cellar also serves as a meeting place for those engaged in less-than-legal dealings. Hidden passages are rumored to lead out of the city, providing a perfect escape for those needing to disappear without a trace.

Though its clientele has changed over the years, the Dancing Pork remains a cornerstone of [[Semper]]'s underbelly, a place where secrets are exchanged as easily as coin.
